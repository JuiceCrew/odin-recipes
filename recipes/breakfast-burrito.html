Breakfast Burrito

Description

A delicious meal loaded with calories and protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 cup Spinach
  • 0.25 cup, shredded Cheddar or Colby Cheese (Lowfat)
  • 1 large Tortilla
  • 1.5 serving Fully Cooked Turkey Sausage Crumbles
  • Bacon Crumble

Steps

  1. In a 12 inch skillet, coat with cooking spray.
    Saute the spinach until tender and remove from skillet
  2. Sprinkle the cheese over the tortilla and add to the skillet
    for 2-3 minutes until lightly browned. Remove from skillet
  3. Scramble the eggs
  4. Assemble burrito by adding the spinach, sausage, bacon, and eggs
    to the warm tortilla. Wrap tightly and slice
