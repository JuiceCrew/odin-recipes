Fazer Shake

Description

A fruity milkshake loaded with calories and protein. Makes for a delightful morning drink

Ingredients

  • 250ml 2% Milk
  • 2.5 scoops Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1.5 scoops Vanilla Whey Protein Powder
  • 30g Oats
  • 2 tbsp Greek Yogurt
  • Handful of Blueberries
  • Handful of Raspberries
  • Handful of Blackberries
  • 1 Banana

Steps

  1. Pour milk into blender and then add ice cream and protein powder
  2. Add oats and greek yogurt
  3. Add berries and top off with the banana split into 4 pieces to make blending easier
  4. Blend on high for 15 seconds, stir, blend for another 15 seconds
  5. Serve
